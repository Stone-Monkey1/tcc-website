About Me

I'm Al Thomason. I founded Thomason Construction Company because I’m passionate about solving design challenges and bringing together a network of dedicated professionals who specialize in woodworking, painting, electrical, plumbing and more.

With over 40 years of experience in the construction industry here in Central Virginia, I’ve done it all – from new construction to remodeling historic homes. I started out carrying plywood and nails but have moved on to managing the construction of entire subdivisions and coordinating multiple projects across the region.

Let Thomason Construction Company handle all your construction needs – whether it’s a new porch, a bathroom remodel, or a brand new dream home.
